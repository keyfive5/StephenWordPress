Three QR codes every restaurant should be printing

A QR sticker costs cents and works around the clock. These are the three links that earn their keep.

1. The menu

On the bag, on the box, at the counter — customers reorder faster when the menu is one scan away, and you never reprint paper menus after a price change.

2. The review page

The best moment to ask for a review is right when the food arrives hot. A "loved it? tell Google" sticker on the lid catches customers at peak happiness.

3. The loyalty signup

Turn delivery-app customers into direct customers. A scan-to-join sticker moves them onto your own list, where the margin lives.
