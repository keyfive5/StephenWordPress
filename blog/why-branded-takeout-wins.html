Why branded takeout packaging wins repeat customers

Every takeout order is free advertising — a simple way to showcase your branding. Here's why that line is more than a slogan.

A takeout bag travels. It rides in cars, sits in office kitchens, lands on family tables. Along the way, an unbranded bag says nothing. A branded one repeats your name to everyone who sees it — and the person who ordered already likes you, which makes them your most credible advertisement.

The repeat-order math

Restaurants live on regulars: raising repeat-visit rates even a few points is worth more than most ad spend. A thank-you sticker with a discount code, a QR label linking to your loyalty signup, or simply a memorable logo seal turns a one-time delivery into a reason to come back.

Start small

You don't need a packaging redesign. One roll of branded stickers upgrades the plain bags, boxes and cups you already buy — for cents per order.
