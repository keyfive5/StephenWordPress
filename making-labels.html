For optimal printing, artwork should be at least 300 DPI.

Accepted file types

Both vector and raster artwork are acceptable. However, for the crispest print quality, vector files are the preferred format, though high resolution raster artwork is also suitable. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, AI, GIF, SVG, BMP, PDF, TIFF, and PSD.

Color information

Computer screens and phones display colors using RGB, while commercial printers use CMYK inks. Because your artwork must be converted from RGB to CMYK for printing, some colors may appear slightly different on the finished label than they do on your screen.
